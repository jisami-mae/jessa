Our Story

Brewing passion since 2010

Welcome to The Roasted Bean. We are dedicated to sourcing the finest coffee beans from around the world and roasting them to perfection right here in our shop.


Whether you need a quick espresso to kickstart your morning or a quiet corner to work in, our doors are always open. We believe coffee is more than just a drink—it's a ritual, a moment of peace, and a community.
